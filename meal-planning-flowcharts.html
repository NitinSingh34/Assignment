Meal Planning User Flows

From Culinary Chaos to Kitchen Confidence

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 The Time-Strapped Parent

"The Sunday Reset" → Weekly Planning Flow

CRISIS MOMENT
Sunday 3pm: Kids napping
20 precious minutes of silence
Opens app → "Quick family setup"
🏠 Chaos Mode: ACTIVATED
"What's your survival level?"
Barely Holding On | Manageable | Got This
📸 Panic-snap fridge contents
🎤 "Ground beef, yogurt cups dying Thursday"
AI Magic: 3 kid-approved meals
+ 2 "emergency pizza alternatives"
Reality Check: Swaps fancy for simple
"Herb-crusted salmon" → "Fish sticks"
📱 Shopping list (only missing stuff)
⏰ Smart reminders: "Defrost before chaos"
Human Truth:
Parents need permission to be imperfect. 3-4 realistic meals + backup plans beats 7 Pinterest-worthy disasters.

💪 The Health-Conscious Professional

"The Panic Moment" → Real-Time SOS

DISASTER STRIKES
6:30pm: Hangry + exhausted
Meal prep = epic fail
Emergency app launch
🚨 "SAVE ME FROM TAKEOUT"
📍 GPS: "You're home (barely)"
⏱️ Energy scan: "30 mins max or I die"
📸 Fridge archaeology expedition
OR "What did I buy last weekend?"
AI calculates: Today's workout intensity
+ Macro goals + Remaining brain cells
"15-min protein power bowl"
Using: frozen salmon + pre-cooked quinoa + bagged greens
⏰ Foolproof timer steps
📊 Macro breakdown + 🛍️ "Order healthy backup"
Post-meal reality check:
"Nailed it!" vs "Too ambitious for Tuesday"
Human Truth:
Even health warriors need an escape plan. The app should normalize ordering smarter, not shame the user into cooking when they're spent.

👫 The Couple With Diverse Diets

"The Negotiation Dance" → Harmony Creation

THE STANDOFF
"What should we eat this week?"
*crickets* *awkward staring*
Partner A (vegetarian) opens shared space
Partner B (carnivore) gets notification
🎨 "This week's vibe board"
Like Tinder, but for food moods
Quick mood tags:
"Comfort food" | "Fresh & light" | "Zero effort"
AI finds the sweet spot:
🌮 "Mexican week = customizable happiness"
Modular meal magic:
Base + A's toppings + B's protein = peace
📝 Smart shopping split:
"Shared cart" + "A's extras" + "B's add-ons"
Sunday team prep:
Together: bases | Solo: personal touches
Daily vibe check:
"Still Mexican-ing?" + pivot options
Human Truth:
Couples don't need identical meals, they need diplomatic solutions. Build bridges, don't force consensus on every single bite.

Crisis Management Flows

😰 Guilt Spiral Recovery

"The Waste Prevention SOS"

SHAME SPIRAL BEGINS
Opens fridge → wilted greens
"I'm terrible at this"
🆘 "Save My Groceries" mode
No judgment, just solutions
📸 Sad vegetable documentation
OR inventory damage assessment
AI triage: "Expires in 1-2 days"
+ upcoming meal compatibility scan
Immediate rescue missions:
"Tonight: spinach pasta" + "Tomorrow: smoothie"
🧊 "Prep & freeze" alternatives
Turn guilt into future convenience
💰 Victory tracking:
"You rescued $23 of groceries!"
Human Truth:
Shame kills motivation. Transform "I failed again" into "I'm learning to adapt" with concrete rescue strategies.

🔋 Energy Check-In

"The Reality Adaptation Flow"

MONDAY TRUTH BOMB
Weekend plans derailed
Energy = 0%
Gentle app ping:
"How's your week looking, human?"
Energy level selector:
"Thriving" | "Surviving" | "Send help"
Auto-downgrade magic:
"Homemade curry" → "Store curry + rice"
Strategic shortcuts activated:
"Rotisserie chicken = 2 meals worth"
Shopping list pivot:
Removes complexity, adds convenience
Permission to be human:
"This week = nourishment over perfection"
Human Truth:
Bad weeks are normal, not failures. The tool should be a supportive friend who helps you maintain dignity while eating well-ish.
